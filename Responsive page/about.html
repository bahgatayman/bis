Our History

Founded in 1995, BIS Zamalek College has established itself as a premier institution for Management Information Systems education in Egypt. With a rich history spanning nearly three decades, we have consistently produced graduates who excel in the dynamic field of information technology and business.

Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between business management and information technology, creating a unique educational experience that prepares students for the challenges of the digital age. Over the years, we have expanded our programs, facilities, and partnerships to maintain our position at the forefront of MIS education.

Today, BIS Zamalek stands as a testament to quality education, innovation, and commitment to student success, with thousands of alumni making significant contributions to organizations across Egypt and beyond.

Our Values

At BIS Zamalek, we are guided by a set of core values that shape our educational philosophy and institutional culture:

  • Excellence: Commitment to the highest standards in teaching, research, and community service
  • Innovation: Fostering creativity and embracing technological advancements
  • Integrity: Upholding ethical standards and academic honesty
  • Collaboration: Promoting teamwork and partnerships with industry
  • Diversity: Creating an inclusive environment that respects different perspectives

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education in Management Information Systems that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing innovative professionals capable of driving digital transformation in organizations. We are committed to fostering research, ethical values, and community engagement to serve Egypt's technological and economic development.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution in Management Information Systems education in the Middle East, known for our academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and successful graduates who lead the digital economy. We aspire to be the preferred choice for students seeking a transformative educational experience in business technology.
