Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of study at the college?

The duration of study at the college is 4 years to obtain a Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems, divided into 8 semesters.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission to the college requires a high school diploma or equivalent with a minimum score of 65%, in addition to passing the personal interview and any admission tests if required.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, the college offers several types of scholarships including academic excellence scholarships, sports scholarships, and scholarships for employees' children. Details can be obtained from the Admissions and Registration Office.

What are the career opportunities for graduates?

Graduates can work in various fields such as systems analysis, database management, business analysis, technical project management, management and technical consulting, and other fields that combine technology and business.

Are there graduate programs available?

Yes, the college offers graduate programs including specialized diplomas and a Master's degree in Management Information Systems. Details can be obtained from the Graduate Studies Department.
